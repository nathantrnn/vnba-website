Design System Overview

Welcome to the VNBA Design System. This system is the single source of truth for our brand identity, UI components, and frontend patterns. It's built to help us create a cohesive, accessible, and high-quality digital experience for the Vietnam Basketball Academy, reinforcing our position as the leading youth basketball training academy in Vietnam.

Consistency & Cohesion

Ensures a unified brand experience across all digital touchpoints by providing a standardized set of visual styles and components.

Efficiency & Scalability

Streamlines the design and development process, allowing for faster iteration and scalable solutions as VNBA grows.

Quality & User Experience

Promotes high standards of usability and accessibility, delivering a premium and intuitive experience for all users.

Color Scheme
